20 bottles
19 have 1g pills
1 has 1.1g pills
1 precise messurement
Which one has 1.1g pills?
=========================

1) Mark bottles 0 .. 19.
2) Take same number of pills from the bottle as it's mark.
3) Weight them all.
4) Subtract sum(0 .. 19) from the result.
5) Divide by 0.1.
6) Result is bottle mark.
